Grateful Dead Recordings

The Grateful Dead performed over 2500 concerts and there are recordings of almost every one!
It can be a little overwhelming trying to get into their live music, with so many options.
In order to make it a little less daunting, I'm including some of my favorite live recordings. Here they are!


My favorite concert recordings

Cornell 77
Many people consider this show to be one of the best of all time. It is great from front to back and is a wonderful showcase of '77 dead
Buffalo 77
This show happened a few days after Cornell. Personally it's my favorite show of all time.
Oregon 72
This is another popular show, incredible from front to back. Highlights include a 30+ minute Dark Star breaking into El Paso. Weird and Wild!
Robert Kennedy Stadium 73
Absolutley ripping show! Great example of "Wall of Sound" era Dead
Alpine Valley Music Theater 89
This is one of my favorite shows from the 80s. It features a different sound than the 70s dead.
